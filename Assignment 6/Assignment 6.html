What Graphic Designer is Like

Graphic-Designer

Brandi Sea Heft-Kniffin

"Design can be a thankless job. It's kind of a kind of invisible job in a lot of ways. People don’t realize that it is all around them every day and even more than that, not many people consider that someone actually created all the design they use, see, and generally take for granted. We truly have much more important jobs than just selling junk people don’t need. Unfortunately, the majority of mankind fails to see that without a designer somewhere, whether sitting in their home office, all alone as the sole designer for a company, or in a room full of other designers at an advertising firm, that their everyday lives would have zero visual direction."
